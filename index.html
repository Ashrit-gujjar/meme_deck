Welcome to DROPDECK

  • Tap + to load a meme sound from your files.
  • Tap a pad once to fire it once. Hold a pad to loop it until you let go.
  • The right-hand deck holds your background track — drag its left edge to resize.
  • Tap the hamburger to open your library — separate Music and BGM's sections, each with its own + to add files.
  • Tap any track in the library to load it onto the deck.
  • Adding new files to the library never interrupts whatever's already playing.
  • Loading a track shows a spinning disk, a progress bar, and its own volume slider.
  • Playing a drop ducks the track to 80% automatically, then brings it back up.
  • Hover any control for a moment to see what it does.
